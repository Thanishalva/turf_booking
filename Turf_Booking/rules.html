The WT TURF Rules

Kindly follow up below turf rules. Any violation in rules will be send out and liable to pay for the damages as stated by the Manager. Please follow and Help us to serve Better!!.

Do's

  • ✓ Use by permit only. No spectators allowed on field at any time
  • ✓ Only regular sports shoes with rubber soles are allowed.
  • ✓ Ensure to Clean all footwear before getting onto the Turf court.
  • ✓ Coaches must carry an appropriate first aid kit and cell phone.
  • ✓ Ensure to Book your timeslot to reserve your spots.
  • ✓ Take care of your belongings. Institution is not responsible for if lost on premise.

Don'ts

  • ✕ Sports shoes with studs or spikes of any kind are not allowed on the TheTurfCity
  • ✕ Guests are not allowed to hang, hold or rest on the nets.
  • ✕ Guests must not use the facility in the absence of proper lighting.
  • ✕ Motorized vehicles/bicycles are not permitted on the turf(except maintenance vehicles)
  • ✕ No Pets/tables or chairs allowed on turf unless mat is placed under equipment.
  • ✕ Before leaving the premises, please return all equipment provided by us.

Note :

Any damage to the facility will incur charges - We reserve the right to refuse entry and remove guests who break any of the above rules - Anyone hiring the TheTurf[City][TTC] facility must adhere to these standard rules and regulations of use.
